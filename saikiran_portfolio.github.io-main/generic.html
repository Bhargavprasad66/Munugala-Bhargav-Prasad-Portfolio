Healthcare Analytics

Executed an end-to-end healthcare analytics project using SQL and Power BI to analyze over 50,000 patient records from hospital databases. Focused on identifying patterns in readmission rates, emergency visits, and length of stay. Used SQL for data extraction and transformation, and Power BI for building dynamic dashboards used by hospital management. Performed correlation analysis between diagnosis types, treatment delays, and patient outcomes. Created visual models to detect bottlenecks in patient discharge workflows. Insights were used to optimize staffing and reduce overcrowding in emergency departments. Key Outcomes: • Reduced average length of stay by 1.2 days in high-traffic departments • Enabled early identification of patients at risk of readmission using trend analysis.

Bank Analytics

Executed a banking analytics project aimed at improving customer retention, credit risk assessment, and operational efficiency. Worked with real-world banking datasets involving customer demographics, transaction history, loan performance, and product usage patterns. Used Power BI and SQL to create interactive dashboards that visualized customer segmentation, loan default trends, and branch-wise performance. Conducted exploratory data analysis (EDA) to identify high-risk customers and detect fraudulent transactions based on spending behavior. Developed KPI dashboards to monitor metrics like customer lifetime value (CLV), loan delinquency rate, NPA ratio, and cross-sell opportunities. Collaborated with stakeholders to translate data insights into actionable strategies, such as personalized product recommendations, improved credit scoring models, and targeted marketing for high-value clients. Tools & Skills Used: SQL, Power BI, Excel, Data Cleaning, Customer Segmentation, Risk Analysis, Banking KPIs, Dashboard Design.
